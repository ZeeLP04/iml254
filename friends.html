WHAT MY FRIENDS MEAN TO ME:

Some people would prefer to be by themselves, but not me. I just love human interaction so much. I guess you could see me as a social butterfly. But then again, there are sometimes I prefer to be by myself. Everything will be possible once you are in the right company, regardless of the circumstances.

In all honestly, it's about the kind of friends that you surround yourself with and choose to stick around for a long time. Lucky for me, I have a large group of people that I'm proud to call my friends. From my high school best friends to my current university friends, I'm just glad that people are also willing to be friends with me.
